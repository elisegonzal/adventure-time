30th Birthday Adventures

Weekend adventures in and around Philly

For when you have some free time and want to use it, but don't want to think - here is a collection of funky little adventures that I think you'd love. You can draw a random one from your cards or choose one from this list, go by yourself or take a pal, and stick to the plan or go totally off course. There are some places here that you know, some I know and I think you'd like, and some that are totally new. Some have adventure subsidies attached to grease the wheels of whimsy.
