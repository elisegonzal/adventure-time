The route

The main event of this adventure day is a hike in Wissahickon. Stretch your legs, overpack water, bring your special hiking pepper spray type of thing. Maybe you grab a coffee beforehand, maybe you grab a beer afterward. It's a very chill nature-y type of day. You'll want to park somewhere around Eremita Cafe. You can walk to the park from there, or drive from there to the Blue Bell Field parking lot.

Your destination: Wissahickon Park
Take a hike! Here's an easy squeezy 2ish mile loop for some fresh air.
