The route

A subway adventure! Take the Broad Street Line south to Snyder, and then walk Passyunk all the way back.
Once you're off the train, Passyunk is just one block north. There are one million little shops and stops on the way, but I picked a few I think you shouldn't miss! Here's a wandering route starting at the Snyder BSL stop..
